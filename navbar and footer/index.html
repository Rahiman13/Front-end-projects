Navbar & Logo

A navbar, short for navigation bar, is a crucial component of a website's user interface. Typically located at the top of a webpage, a navbar serves as a navigation menu that helps users easily access different sections of the site. It often includes links to essential pages, such as home, about, services, and contact. Additionally, a navbar may incorporate interactive elements like dropdown menus, search bars, and buttons, enhancing the user experience and providing a clear pathway for visitors to navigate through the website.
